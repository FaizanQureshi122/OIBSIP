Steve Jobs

Biography

Steve Jobs was an American entrepreneur, business magnate, inventor, and industrial designer. He was the co-founder, chairman, and chief executive officer (CEO) of Apple Inc., among other ventures.

Jobs is widely recognized as a pioneer of the personal computer revolution of the 1970s and 1980s, along with Apple co-founder Steve Wozniak. He was also a central figure in the development of the Macintosh, iPod, iPhone, and iPad.
